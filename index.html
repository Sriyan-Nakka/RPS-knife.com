Game Rules

Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors, Knife. This is a modification of the classic game rock, paper, scissors. Let me explain the rules.

The game is just like Rock, Paper, Scissors, but the twist is that there is a knife. You need to assign a goal with the minimum being 3 and the maximum value being 25. When you are very low on points and feel like you need to win the game, you can use the knife.

When you use the knife, the opponent needs to guess of a number that you are think of between 1 and 2. If the opponent guesses wrong, they are killed and you win. But, if they guess the number correctly, they are not killed and the bot gets a point, which makes using the knife a high risk high reward, which makes it balanced and not very overpowered.

Note: The knife can only be used at a certain time, it will not help you early on in the game and will only appear when you and your opponent start to gain points to reach the desired goal. And your opponent AKA The bot cannot use the knife.

Good luck and have fun!
